About Us

Our society lacks an all-in-one space for the less integrated local community,
living in a large or small city and who are not able to access the available services. It is even more
unlikely to access the best and the most deserving ones who - as per definition - are the most reliable,
talented, and passionate. On the other hand, most local professionals fail to provide access to information
and online presence in English. As a result, a “simple and quick” Google search suddenly becomes
a “frustrating and lengthy” task. Language barriers together with the unfamiliarity with the environment
will lead you to inaccurate results, and this is only the beginning of a tunnel of desperation and delusion.
Jobidy's purpose is to leave time-consuming Google searches in the past by creating an all-in-one marketplace
where people in need and service providers can meet, agree on a job, agree on a price, and maybe become
best friends. Or just friends. It is up to you. Anyway, you may be wondering how we are going to do this, right?
Well, [here] you can watch the video where we explain in just 30 seconds how this magic works.
